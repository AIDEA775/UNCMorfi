Why is the menu not updated?

We get the menu from the university's website:

  • www.unc.edu.ar/vida-estudiantil/menú-de-la-semana

If the menu doesn't refresh, it is be because the university did not update it.

Where do they get Balance and Meter information?

From the university dining room's website:

  • tBalance: comedor.unc.edu.ar/autoconsulta.php
  • tMeter: comedor.unc.edu.ar/cocina.php

Why are Internet and camera permissions needed?

  • Internet access is necessary for updating the menu, the balance and the measurer.
  • Camera access is for adding the card by scanning the barcode on it.

Do I need Internet to check my card balance?

All the data is stored on the device to check offline, but you need to be connected to the Internet to get it updated.

Do I can eat twice in one day?

No.

What is the bell that rings every so often in the dining room?

Legend has it that when the bell rings there is rotation among the cooks.
